My name is Marc. I'm 22. Originally from Singapore, I've spent most of my life on the West Coast of Canada. Currently, I'm in the final stretch of my undergraduate studies at the University of Victoria, pursuing an honours degree in mathematics. My academic interests lie in mathematics, physics, computer science and philosophy. I'm particularly fascinated by how these fields intersect, especially in cognitive science and learning.

Outside of that, I enjoy resistance training, cooking, chess, and playing the piano and guitar. I also appreciate spending time outdoors, and through photography, I try to capture the feeling of a peaceful landscape and the busy nature of everyday life in similar spirits. I write about some of my hobbies and interests, among other things, here.
