Brief about me

My name is Marc. I'm 22. I'm originally from Singapore, but I've spent most of my life on the West Coast of Canada. Currently, I'm in the final stretch of my undergraduate studies at the University of Victoria, pursuing an honours degree in mathematics. My academic interests lie in mathematics, physics, computer science and philosophy. I'm particularly fascinated by how these fields intersect, especially in cognitive science and learning.

Outside of that, I enjoy the gym, cooking, chess, and playing the piano and guitar. I also enjoy nature and like spending time outdoors, shooting photographs.
